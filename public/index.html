Timestamp Microservice

Usage:

Append "natural date" to the root https://domain/<naturaldate> and receive a unix timestamp

Append "unix timestamp" to the root https://domain/<timestamp> and receive a natural date

Example:

https://domain/December 15, 2015
https://domain/1450166400

Result:

{ "unix": 1450166400, "natural": "Tue Dec 15 2015" }
